Web design

Aseo Eco Bambu

Eco bamboo was born at the end of 2020 through the entrepreneurial idea of a group of university students who were looking for of new business opportunities saw it pertinent to delve into one of the problems that most afflicts to the world today; which is the pollution of non-recyclable plastics. Eco bamboo through production of dispensers and refillable containers that are manufactured from a base derived from bamboo fibers, aims to mitigate the consequences of single-use plastics.

For the creation of the website, the arrangement of the brand's corporate colors was taken into account and based on In this, a main layout was generated with a navigation bar, a minimalist environment arranged by the main color palette in combination with the absence of color, within the provisions relevant within the design, a logical order was made within each section. Which allowed not Friction will occur within the arrangements of the information and the objects presented, within each segment.

Within the layouts of the page, justification parameters, color palette and typography were used. homogeneous, which allowed us to have an identity and coherence within the design of the website. Having present in turn the composition by blocks, with which each of the information segments was arranged and points of interaction with the user. The delimitation of the contents according to the visualization was kept in mind of the users and devices used to view the contents; whether web, tablet or smartphone.

The font used throughout the website is "Poppins, sans-serif". A font geometric Sans-Serif that is, without serif or serif, which allows us to evoke an effect of modernity, minimalism, cleanliness and, above all, all allow easy viewing of information, no matter what device it is being viewed on. To the Being a source with 5 different weights allows us to vary with the strength and intensity of what we transmit, something that we try to adapt within the weight of the initial title, the titles according to each segment and the texts.

Keeping in mind that the structure of the page aims to awaken within users an image of cleanliness and minimalism, but at the same time very linked to what “Eco Bambu Toilet” meant. Some mockups are made that complete the image of the brand, where what is provided within the web design is complementary to the graphic samples that reinforce the visual of the brand within the page.

To complement the aesthetics of the website, scroll is used to generate previews of scrolling content, in addition to several aesthetic complements for previewing buttons, points of interaction, scroll buttons and search bar. All these resources were implemented within classes specific, which allowed us to replicate processes and standardize interaction functions within the site, without affecting its functionality.

The relevance of the banners on the website are fundamental for the initial and final presentation of what “Aseo Eco Bambu” wants to convey to its users through its message. That is why when creating the banner At the beginning of the page, a more complex interface with transitions and objects was available; focused on the goal of the company and its environmental commitment. And in the final banner we find a more sober interface, focused on presenting a general summary of the website and its contact information.

Within the mechanisms created for the website, the “contact us” section was implemented. Which will allow receiving and sending messages. And their respective conglomeration of information in SQL bases. This integration, Although it was implemented in the code using php and its classes defined for HTML5, it depends largely on the cpanel interface with its tools and its respective connection with the database. However, this section is of great value, since it allows generating volumetrics and data on user satisfaction regarding the “Aseo Eco Bambu” brand.
