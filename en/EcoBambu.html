Brand Design

Aseo Eco Bambu

Eco Bamboo Toilet was born at the end of 2020 through the entrepreneurial idea of a group of university students who were looking for of new business opportunities saw it pertinent to delve into one of the problems that most afflicts to the world today; which is the pollution of non-recyclable plastics. Eco bamboo through production of dispensers and refillable containers that are manufactured from a base derived from bamboo fibers, aims to mitigate the consequences of single-use plastics.

For the creation of the logo, the natural fibers of bamboo are taken as a structural precedent as a base in the design composition. To do this, a 45° base grid is established, the typography “Poppins, sans-serif” as initial basis of the modeling and the palette designed for this brand composed of the three base colors of this composition. By having these basic components, the brand is structured using the Poppins font. Without However, it is only a basis for the initial modeling, which would later be diagrammed with a larger image. minimalist, clean and friendly.

The color arrangement within the logo is arranged by a linear gradient, in which an emphasis was placed in the two main colors of the palette. Maintained a solid color in the first two words and a gradient linear in the other two words; This allowed generating a color contrast within the design, generating a differentiation and visual strength between the elements of the logo.

The colors selected for the design proposal are influenced by the colors of bamboo and their intervals, within a proposal that would reflect the identity of “Aseo Eco Bambú”. That is why it is taken bamboo, one of the main components within the “Aseo Eco Bambú” products as a driving axis between the meaning per se and its qualities as a flexible and ecological material.

The color palette used within the design is structured by three colors, which are located within a color range. Mainly composed of a green color palette as the protagonist, which evokes tranquility, nature and ecology. In addition to a chromatic composition that interspersed the color main with the others, which allowed us a homogeneous arrangement between the other colors of the palette.

The font used is "Poppins, sans-serif". A Sans-Serif geometric typeface, that is, without a serif or dry stick, which allows us to evoke an effect of modernity, minimalism, cleanliness and above all allow easy visualization of information. Being a source with 5 different weights, it allows us to vary the strength and intensity of what we transmit. That is why, for the design proposal, Poppins Bold, Black was used and Medium. For the initial layouts of the logo sketch and for the consequent reorganizations of the content and information within the design of the proposal.

Keeping in mind the provisions already established within the brand design, some archetypes are made that complete the “Eco Bambú Toilet” proposal. That is why two prototypes are modeled that represent the initial brand proposal; in which its ergonomics, design, replication in a process were taken into account productive and its environmental impact.

By establishing two prototypes for the “Eco Bambú Toilet” proposal. It is defined that the design, although it will be simple; It will be focused on being a functional product that is easy to use for those who produce it and friendly for those who produce it. uses in your daily life. For this reason, the two proposed models have characteristics that evoke the identity of the brand, without overloading the product with graphic references.

By prioritizing functionality and having a sober and minimalist design. There was greater scope for the presentation of “Aseo Eco Bambú” as a brand within the market.
