Alianza Service

To perform the Alianza Service analysis. A main interaction structure was created where control buttons for entering the raw materials at the bottom and other buttons on the right side focused on the entry and reporting. This entire interface is linked to an interaction using the show function to display forms, views and surveys created using macros; These macros focus on the intermediate view of the general visuals of the tool for Alianza Service.

Within the data analysis visual, visuals are created automatically using macros linked to the three raw materials available (customers, sales and products). These three groups are unified through power pivot and generated automatically in the “Analysis” Sheet using the macro linked to the “Report Generator” button. Within the Visuals generated for each section, bar graphs are generated to compare performance on a quarterly basis. of the sales of the top products that have sold the most and the types of customers by gender and age. In addition to its corresponding comparison according to their categories and types of products.
