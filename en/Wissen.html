Web design

Wissen

Currently, the number of young people who do not clearly know what professional and work career to start after completing their school studies it is very low and according to studies around 70% of Students graduate from their schools without receiving adequate vocational guidance. This problem It is one of the most influential factors in university dropout and there are very few applications competent to provide a solution to the problem raised. It is for this reason that Wissen was born, a mobile application that will provide comprehensive services for vocational guidance and support of young people in the tenth and eleventh grades of the city of Bogotá.

For the structuring of the website, the arrangement of the brand's corporate colors, the which tried to stand out with a color proposal that was adverse to other applications within the mobile applications focused on education. Based on this, a main layout was generated with a bar navigation, a structured environment with different scrolls within the main blocks of information; allowing you to maximize what is available in a dynamic and simple way. All this allowed us to present the information holistically without generating friction within the arrangements of the information and objects presented, within each segment.

Within the layouts of the page, justification parameters, color palette and typography were used. homogeneous, which allowed us to have an identity and coherence within the design of the website. Having present in turn the composition by blocks, with which each of the information segments was arranged and points of interaction with the user. The delimitation of the contents according to the visualization was kept in mind of the users and devices used to view the contents; whether web, tablet or smartphone.

The font used throughout the website is "Barlow, sans-serif." A geometric Sans-Serif typeface that is, without serif or serif, which allows us to evoke an effect of modernity, minimalism and professionalism. In addition to being an appropriate font to display information, it allows us to have easy reading of the information. and thanks to the two intervals within the weight of the typography we can define guidelines within each section, which allows better compression for the reader.

The structure of the website in its essence is composed of a purely academic approach, where the design and its Information visualization is defined by Wissen's value proposition. However, although the proposal The design is aligned with the vision and mission, in this case greater relevance is given to the information within the website.

To support the present information, some mockups were made to complete the brand image, where the provisions Within the design of the website, be complementary with graphic samples that reinforce the visual of the brand within the page and in turn generate new dynamics to present the information.

The segmentation of information throughout the Wissen website is one of the fundamental components, since the Information was structured by functional segments where the general order was respected, the relevance of the information It was taken according to the functional positioning and the data were homogeneously united with the visual resources.

Being differentiating within the market and having similar functional characteristics were base components for the structuring of segments within the website; since the relevance of each component depended on familiarity of users with the mechanisms in place.

Within the mechanisms created for the website, the “Pre-Beta” section was implemented. Which will allow the receipt of contact information to enter the Pre-Beta of the Wissen application. Data entry by this mechanism will be stored within SQL databases in text formats. This integration, although it was implemented in the code using php and its classes defined for HTML5, depends largely on the cpanel interface with its tools and their respective connection with the database.

According to what was stated in the design proposal of the Wissen Web, we can determine that a route of modeling of the site where the brand Briefing was captured, a UI prototype of the website and the application using Adobe XD. Capturing the identity of the brand within the two models and at the same time making a in-depth analysis of Wissen's business vision as an actor within the Colombian environment.
